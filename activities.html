Women in Computer Science, Statistics, and Mathematics

Sept 2019 - Present

Position: Mentor

As a part of the first ever mentorship program with WiCSM, we aim to assist first-year female computer science, mathematics, and statistics students feel a sense of community within the CMS deparment.

Our role is to provide mentor support the students in many different aspects:

  • resume and interview prep
  • exam prep
  • study habits
  • course and program selection
  • first year advice
  • campus tours
  • resources on campus and online

To do our duties, we stay connected to the mentees through social events and bi-weekly mentor and mentee meetings, scheduled by the mentors. Communication are also held outside of phyiscal meetings, to assist in resolving needs for immediate help.

Student Ambassador

Oct 2018 - Present

Position: Tour Guide and Event Staff

Events:

  • Mar 23, 2019 (March Showcase) - Tour Guide
  • Nov 10, 2018 (Fall Campus Day) - Event Staff

Starting out as an event staff in first year, I helped set up tear tear down for major events. With a mass volume of people, I guided and clarified inquiries about campus resources, history, university programs, and general university inquires.

After passing a training session to become a tour guide, I guided prospective students and parents through campus in groups of 5 or more people for 45-minute tours. Tours include knowledge of:
